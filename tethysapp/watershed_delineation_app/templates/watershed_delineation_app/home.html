Welcome to Watershed Delineation for DR App!

This app allows you to perform watershed delineation in Dominican Republic Country.

You can zoom in/out of the map to find an outlet, then click on the map to draw a point. Then you can click "Delineate Watershed" button. The app will snap the outlet to the nearest stream, and delineate watershed. Resulted watershed and snapped point will show on the map.

Please make sure to select an outlet within the Dominican Republic Country.
